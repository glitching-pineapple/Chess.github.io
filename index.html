How we work

Our programs are flexible and responsive to community needs. Whether a school wants help launching a chess club, a youth program needs volunteers, or students need free tutoring — we provide thoughtful, practical, locally-grounded support.

We see chess not only as a game, but as a platform for academic growth, mentorship, and stronger community ties.

"Chess teaches patience, problem-solving, and the power of thinking ahead — skills that matter far beyond the board."

Programs

Designed to grow skills,
leadership, and community.

From first-time players to tournament competitors — we have programming for every stage of the journey.

Program 01

Free Chess Tutoring

One-on-one and small-group instruction that builds confidence, teaches core strategy, and supports consistent improvement.

Program 02

School Chess Clubs

Support for starting and sustaining school-based chess communities where students learn, practice, and grow together.

Program 03

Community Workshops

Introductory and themed workshops for schools, libraries, and youth spaces that make chess welcoming and fun.

Program 04

Tournament Mentorship

Guidance for students entering competitive play — preparation, strategy development, and structured encouragement.
