Chess Outreach Initiative

A student-led effort focused on expanding access to chess education through free tutoring, school programs, and community outreach in underrepresented communities.

Mission

Our mission is to expand access to high-quality chess instruction by providing free tutoring programs and school partnerships in communities with limited access to enrichment opportunities.

Through structured lessons, mentorship, and competition preparation, we aim to use chess as a tool to develop critical thinking, patience, and confidence among students.

Programs

  • Free chess tutoring for elementary and middle school students
  • School chess club mentorship and curriculum support
  • Community chess workshops in underserved neighborhoods
  • Volunteer-led tournament preparation and coaching
