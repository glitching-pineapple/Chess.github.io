Student-led chess access and mentorship

Open Square brings chess education to students who deserve more opportunities.

Open Square is a student-led organization focused on expanding access to free chess tutoring, school clubs, and community programming. We use chess to build confidence, strategic thinking, and long-term academic growth in communities that are often overlooked by traditional enrichment programs.

Why the identity works

Logo concept

The outer square represents structure and access. The inner square suggests an open board space, a move, or a door opening to opportunity.

Color palette

Deep navy creates credibility, amber adds warmth and visibility, and blue acts as a clean modern accent. This gives the site stronger contrast than the earlier white-and-teal version.

Mission

Using chess as a tool for learning, confidence, and belonging.

Chess can be far more than a game. It teaches discipline, patience, decision-making, and long-term thinking. Open Square partners with schools and community organizations to make those benefits available to more students.

What we believe

Every student deserves access to meaningful enrichment. We focus on communities where high-quality extracurricular opportunities are limited, especially across Oakland and the East Bay.
