Expanding access to chess education.

We are a student-led initiative focused on bringing free chess tutoring and mentorship to schools and communities that have limited access to enrichment programs. Our goal is to use chess to build critical thinking, confidence, and opportunity.

Mission

Chess is one of the most powerful educational tools for developing discipline, patience, and analytical thinking. Our initiative partners with schools and community organizations to provide structured chess instruction, mentorship, and club support.

We focus especially on expanding access in underrepresented and low-income communities across the East Bay, including Oakland, where enrichment programs are often limited.

Programs

  • Free chess tutoring for elementary and middle school students
  • Volunteer-led school chess clubs
  • Community chess workshops and outreach events
  • Tournament preparation and mentorship
